How ScholarLens works

Three simple steps from data to direction.

1

Enter your academic data

Input your current CGPA, target CGPA, weekly study hours, attendance rate, and number of carry-overs in our clean dashboard form.

2

Our model runs the analysis

A multivariate linear regression model trained on real-world student data computes your predicted CGPA and risk level in milliseconds.

3

Get your personalised action plan

See the exact gap between your predicted and target CGPA, plus specific, measurable steps to close that gap before your next semester.
